Tournaments

Shatranj

Shatranj is the biggest chess tournament held during our annual college fest Pyrokinesis. This tournament is open for all, so participants come at big numbers from various parts of Assam

Freshers Tournament

Freshers Tournament is organised for the Freshers batch who are interested in joining the club. We also organise a workshop before the tournament where we teach all the beginers how to play the game.

AEC pro chess league

Pro Chess League is a series we organise for the members of the club.In this series of tournaments which we organise each month even alumnis can also participate.We make a leader board where update the points earned by each player in each tournament. We announce the winners of each tournament in our social media handles and at the end of the series we give prizes to the top 5 winners during our Shatranj event in pyrokinesis.
